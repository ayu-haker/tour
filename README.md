# 🗺️ Smart City & Tour Guide App

The **Smart City & Tour Guide App** is a multipurpose travel and city utility platform designed to make urban exploration easier, smarter, and more connected.  
It helps users **discover tourist attractions, plan trips, find emergency services, book cabs, and explore nearby food and hospital facilities — all in one place.**

---

## 🌟 Features

- 🏛️ **Tourist Attractions:**  
  Discover famous places with images, ticket details, timings, and directions.

- 🚖 **Cab Booking:**  
  Find and book rides for smooth travel across the city.

- 🍔 **Food & Restaurants:**  
  Explore nearby restaurants and cafes with ratings and menus.

- 🏥 **Hospitals & Pharmacies:**  
  Locate emergency services and nearby health centers instantly.

- 🚌 **Public Transport Routes:**  
  Get details on local bus, metro, or train routes for budget travel.

- 💸 **Budget Planner:**  
  Plan your travel expenses and keep track of your trip costs.

- ⭐ **User Reviews & Ratings:**  
  Share your experiences and read other travelers’ reviews.

- 🔒 **Login & Profiles:**  
  Firebase-based secure sign-in for a personalized experience.

- 🧭 **Smart Navigation:**  
  Integrated Google Maps navigation for real-time directions.
